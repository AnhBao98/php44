I like to go swimming in the summer.

Swimming is my most favorite activity in the summer. When the sun is shining
and the air is warm, you will find me dripping into my backyard poll. It's not an
impressive pool, only three feets deep, but it's mine.

There are three reason I like to swim:

  • I get lots of exercise
  • I enjoy the freedom
  • I have an opportunity to be in the sun.
